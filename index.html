Tours

Plan a Visit:

Hours of Operation: Museum exhibits and the Museum Store are open five days a week, 9:00am to 4:00pm.

We recommend that you allow a minimum of 2 hours to view exhibits.
All venues are closed: New Year's Day, July 4th, Mardi Gras Day, Thanksgiving Day, Christmas Eve and Christmas Day.

The Museum is fully accessible to all visitors. A limited number of wheelchairs are provided for use in the Museum at no charge.

If you would like to schedule other stops in Northeast Louisiana or view lodging options please visit the Monroe and West Monroe Convention Visitor's Bureau Website here: Visit Website
